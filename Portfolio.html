I'm a UX & Product Designer based in Germany, studying at the School of Design, Media & IT. I discovered my passion for design near the end of my studies — and haven't looked back since.

I believe the best design is invisible. Products people don't think about, because they just work. I start every project by asking "why" until I understand the problem deeply, then I work across the full stack — research, information architecture, interaction, and visual design — to deliver something both beautiful and purposeful.

My work spans AI platforms, EdTech, and Civic Tech — spaces where thoughtful design has real impact on people's lives. I'm drawn to complex, meaningful problems, especially where technology and human behaviour intersect.

Outside Figma, I'm a photographer. Seeing the world through a lens has made me a better designer — it trains you to notice light, composition, and the stories hidden in everyday moments.
