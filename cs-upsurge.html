About the Project

What is Upsurge?

Upsurge is a family-focused financial platform that offers financial education for children through interactive educational content, games, and gamified real-life responsibility management.

Challenges

Two Phases, One Mission

Over 8 months, I tackled two distinct design challenges — first the website, then the mobile app — each with its own goals and constraints.

Phase 01 · Months 1–4

Website Redesign

Focused on improving the upsurge website across both mobile and desktop. Studied the existing design to make it easier and more enjoyable to use.

Phase 02 · Months 5–8

Mobile App Redesign

The mobile application was not properly designed or prioritised. All elements were outdated and misaligned with the platform's values. Full redesign from ground up.

Phase 01 — Website

Website Design Goals

01 Reduce scroll time for landing page (Mobile version)
02 Revisit Pre-signup Web Elements for better conversion
03 Redesign the full Signup Experience
04 Redesign Post-sign in Experience (Dashboard)
Process — Website

How I Approached It

R

Research & Analysis

Based on previous analysis about scroll time and user retention, I started working on landing page elements. Screenshots of the current interface were collected and all components were recreated to deeply study the existing design.

H

Heuristic Evaluation

While redesigning, I applied the laws of heuristic evaluation on website elements to understand the drawbacks in the current design system.

IA

Information Architecture

Based on research findings, I restructured the website's navigation and content, prioritising features and information according to user needs.

Phase 02 — Mobile App

Mobile App Redesign Goals

01 Redesign the whole mobile experience from scratch
02 Add gamification elements to drive engagement and retention
03 Make it truly kids-friendly — vibrant, accessible, and joyful
Upsurge isnger users.on

Added gamification elements to encourage kids to spend more time and continue learning while earning rewards — badges, streaks, and interactive milestones.

Takeaways

Conclusion

My 8-month journey at Upsurge has been a continuous learning experience. From website designs to crafting gamified mobile experiences, I expanded my skills and knowledge in UX design. As I reflect on this journey, I'm not just closing a chapter but carrying forward a wealth of insights and lessons that will undoubtedly shape my future endeavours in UX design.
