Xane AI

AI-driven Automobile / Appliances Platform

Oct 2024 – Jan 2025 #WebsiteRedesign #AI #UXDesign #Automobiles
About XANE.AI

Xane AI is a B2B SaaS platform that leverages Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to enhance user experiences and streamline business processes. It helps organizations automate workflows, improve efficiency, and reduce costs while delivering personalized interactions.

Overview

Website Redesign (Collaborated with Hari Gopal Rastogi — Founder of Nagrik): This project involved redesigning a website for an AI platform that had an outdated interface and lacked modern usability standards. The redesign aimed to enhance user experience, improve aesthetics, and introduce intuitive navigation.

Website Design Exploration (Goals)
  • Redesign Website Experience
  • Responsive Design
  • Make content/vision Clear
  • Work on color palette
Conclusion

Through this project, I deepened my understanding of micro-interactions and animations, incorporating them to create a more engaging and dynamic user experience. I learned a new tool named Jitter Video during this work opportunity.
