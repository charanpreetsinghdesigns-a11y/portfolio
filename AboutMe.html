About

CHARANPREET SINGH

I discovered my passion for UX design towards the end of my graduation. With a strong interest in design, I was drawn to subjects like research design, design thinking, and principles of information systems during college. I believe that, in today's world, where Gen Z leads the market and AI is reshaping how we interact with technology, aesthetics are just as crucial as functionality. With people's attention span shrinking, I aim to make a strong first impression through thoughtful, appealing designs that leverage both human intuition and AI-powered insights.

I enjoy diving into existing process flows, spotting pain points, and creating solutions that make a meaningful difference. My expertise lies in both UI and UX design, and I'm increasingly exploring how AI tools can accelerate design workflows and enhance user experiences. I aspire to be an impactful designer who contributes to education and supports socially disadvantaged communities—using technology, including AI, as a force for good. Outside of design, I love capturing moments through photography and exploring new places, which further fuels my creativity and helps me see the world through different perspectives.
