Personal Case Study

Teacher Hiring & Upskilling Platform

Feb – Mar 2023 #AppDesign #Edtech #UXDesign #Research
Overview

In Class 12 (School) we faced a challenge in finding a stable Computer Science teacher. The previous teacher left due to personal reasons, and subsequent replacements also left after a few weeks. It always took a month or more for new teacher to join. This cycle repeated itself four times, resulting in 6-7 months of waiting for a teacher. It affected our studies.

This motivated me to think of some solution to the problem and eventually after completing my Certification in UX design, this became my First UX Case Study.

Process

Research: I started this case study with an idea in my head and while doing research about the problems faced by schools in hiring process. Competitive analysis, market research, primary and secondary research was performed.

Modeling: Based on research, affinity mapping was performed and I also looked into the current teacher hiring process.

Requirements: After clear knowledge on what are the drawbacks of current process, I introduced user personas and scenarios.

Framework: This was the time to build Information architecture and understand user flows. Card-sorting was performed to understand user needs.

Detailed Design: In the final step, I wireframed ideas to create an app for hiring and upskilling teachers.

Requirements
Problem Statement

Hiring a Skilled, as per requirement teacher on immediate basis is a concern for schools while recruiting. Further, the existing process of hiring teacher is time-consuming. Good teaching methods are somewhere lacking in the Indian education system.

Vision Statement

A Platform that saves the data of teachers who are open for jobs. Provides certifications in enhancing teacher skills and making it easier for Schools to hire teachers and saving the time, was used for advertising and waiting for a candidate.
