The Origin Story

Where This Began

In Class 12, we faced a challenge finding a stable Computer Science teacher. The previous teacher left due to personal reasons, and subsequent replacements also left after a few weeks. It always took a month or more for a new teacher to join — this cycle repeated four times, resulting in 6–7 months without a teacher. It affected our studies.

This motivated me to think of a solution. Eventually, after completing my UX Design certification, this became my first UX Case Study.

01 Research

Secondary Research — Key Stats

14.6%

Dropout Rate at Secondary level (9–10) as per 2020–21 data

35% / 21%

Boys / Girls who dropped out citing lack of interest in studies

NEP 2020

New Education Policy mandating holistic, student-centred learning

Market Research — Competitors

Helps teachers find job advertisements and schools to post listings — but lacks quality matching and skills verification.

Connects teachers to students for tutoring — but not designed for formal school hiring pipelines.

Advertising-based platform for teacher hiring — passive, slow, and without urgency matching.

General-purpose job platform — not tailored for school hiring or teacher upskilling needs.

02 Modeling

Research Insights

  • Education System needs major changes to make students' interest in studies
  • Curriculum, holistic approach and student-centred learning are today's needs
  • School Infrastructure and Teachers are pillars of quality education
  • Teacher Hiring in school needs time-saving processes
  • Fulfilling urgent requirements fast is the core pain point
  • Improving quality of teachers hired
  • Teacher education and continuous upskilling is essential
  • New teaching methods must be incorporated
  • Improving student-teacher ratio in underserved schools
03 Requirements
Problem Statement

Hiring a skilled teacher matching exact requirements on an immediate basis is a concern for schools. The existing process is time-consuming and good teaching methods are somewhere lacking in the education system.

Vision Statement

A platform that saves data of teachers open for jobs. Provides certifications to enhance teacher skills and makes it easier for schools to hire teachers — saving time previously spent on advertising and waiting.

Conclusion
